# Chapter 1 - Network Basics

## What is a Network?

A network is a group of connected devices that communicate and share resources.

Examples:
- Computers
- Phones
- Servers
- Printers

## Why Networks Are Needed

- Communication
- File Sharing
- Internet Access
- Resource Sharing

## Types of Networks

### LAN (Local Area Network)
Small network in homes, schools, offices.

### WAN (Wide Area Network)
Large network connecting cities and countries.

## Internet

A global network connecting millions of devices.

## ISP

Internet Service Provider.

Examples:
- Jio
- Airtel
- BSNL
